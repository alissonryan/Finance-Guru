## Executive Summary

**Strategy**: Deploy 100% of W2 income to dividend portfolio while using margin to cover living expenses. As dividend income grows, it replaces margin draws and eventually pays off accumulated debt.

**Objective**: Achieve financial independence where dividend income covers all expenses without touching business income or principal.

- Eventually push ALL expenses through margin
- Business income remains untouched safety net

## Risk Management & Guardrails

### **Margin Safety Limits**

**Stop-Loss Triggers:**
- Portfolio drops 25% → STOP new margin draws
- Margin balance exceeds $100K → Reassess strategy
- Business income stops entirely → Use business savings to cover expenses

**Monthly Review Checklist:**
- [ ] Portfolio value vs margin balance ratio (should be 3:1 minimum)
- [ ] Dividend income growth on track (targeting 30% annual yield)
- [ ] Margin interest rate (watch for Fed rate changes)
- [ ] Business income stability (safety net check)

### **Portfolio Protection**

**Never Touch Growth Layer:**
- PLTR: $68,576 (35.71%)
- TSLA: $44,425 (23.13%)
- All other Layer 1 holdings
- These continue compounding untouched

**Margin Collateral:**
- Use dividend portfolio as collateral ONLY
- Never risk selling growth positions for margin calls
- Maintain 50% margin cushion minimum
