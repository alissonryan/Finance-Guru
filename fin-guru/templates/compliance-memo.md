## Items Reviewed - Compliance Checklist

### ✅ Required Disclaimers
- [ ] **Educational Analysis Disclaimer**: "This is educational analysis, not personalized investment advice"
- [ ] **Performance Disclaimer**: "Past performance does not guarantee future results"
- [ ] **Professional Advice Disclaimer**: "Consult a licensed advisor before acting"
- [ ] **Risk Disclosure**: "Invest only what you can afford to lose"

### ✅ Data Handling & Privacy
- [ ] **Sensitive Data Check**: No account numbers, SSNs, or personal identifiers included
- [ ] **Source Attribution**: All data sources properly cited with timestamps
- [ ] **Assumption Documentation**: Key assumptions explicitly stated
- [ ] **Data Quality Notes**: Limitations and gaps appropriately disclosed

### ✅ Risk Framework Compliance
- [ ] **VaR Assessment**: 95% and 99% VaR calculated for 1d/1w/1m periods
- [ ] **Stress Testing**: 2008/2020-style shock scenarios included
- [ ] **Liquidity Analysis**: ADV, spreads, market impact assessed
- [ ] **Diversification Review**: Concentration and correlation risks evaluated
- [ ] **Margin Safety**: Buffer calculations and liquidation paths documented

### ✅ Content Standards
- [ ] **Methodology Transparency**: Analysis approach clearly explained
- [ ] **Scenario Coverage**: Bull/base/bear cases presented
- [ ] **Implementation Clarity**: Step-by-step execution plan provided
- [ ] **Monitoring Framework**: Triggers and rebalancing rules defined
